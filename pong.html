Pong-reloaded

Specifications

  • Duration: Made in 3 days.
  • Platform: PC
  • Type: Solo project

Concept

A 2 player game of ping-pong with extra features to make the game more fun!

Features

  • Main menu UI.
  • Pause menu UI. Can press escape to pause. Resumes with slow-mo.
  • Score counter.
  • Velocity of ball increases with the rally.
  • When scoring streak reaches 3, fire-balls are launched at the opponent. Fire-balls must be dodged.
  • Camera shake and explosion particle effects when fireball hits any of the players.
  • Various SFX bring life to the game.
