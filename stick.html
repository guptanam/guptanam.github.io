Stick it

Specifications

  • Duration: March 2018 - October 2018.
  • Team size: 2
  • Platform: Mobile(android).
  • Type: Solo project

Concept

Stick it is a simple 2D-platformer-endless-runner mobile(andorid) game. It has a sober and minimalistic art to it and appeals to the player. Players will use basic screen-touch gestures to play the game. For example screen-tap to jump and swipe-up/left/down/right to change direction of gravity according to orientation of the platforms. The longer you survive, the more you score.


Key Gameplay Features

  • The change in direction of gravity upon swipe gesture.
  • Camera movement.
  • Procedural generation of the level.
  • Unity's 2D Lightweight Render Pipeline.
