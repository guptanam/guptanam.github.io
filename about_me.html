In 1997, I saw the first light of day. Twenty-three years later here I am pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at University of Southern California. In 2019, I received my Bachelors degree in CS at KIIT, Odisha, India. I've been learning and honing my Game development skills for the past 5 years now, and I'd like to believe that I've gotten pretty good at it. Over the years I developed tremendous interest and curiostiy towards computers, particularly video games. I always wondered how they were made. That curiosity turned me into what I am today, a creative and motivated professional, always eager to learn more tricks of the trade.

I'm also a proficient software developer with working knowledge of various programming languages including Python, C++, C# and JavaScript. Besides, I'm well-versed with Machine learning and Reinforcement learning and have done quite a few projects in this area.

I've learnt many things along the way, gathered new insights and different perspectives. Explored new technologies, new tools, met new people, improved my programming abilites. In my spare time, I sometimes work on personal projects, varying from commercial projects to small hobby projects - either way, I'm always learning.
