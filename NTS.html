Night to survive

Specifications

  • Duration: March 1st - March 2nd 2020 (48 hours)
  • Team size: 4
  • Platform: PC
  • Type: MEGA Game Jam.
  • Secured 2nd place for 'Best Gameplay' and 1st place for 'Best audio effects' among 11 teams.

Concept

Night to Survive is a small-scale horror/thriller game. The story revolves around a person (player) who is trapped in the middle of a forest. He is sheltered in the woods and has a single source of light to keep the zombies away. In order to call for rescue, he needs to fix a radio. But there's a catch. The tools that can fix this piece of equipment are lost somewhere in the forest. The player needs to find these parts and not get caught by the zombies. It's do or die!
