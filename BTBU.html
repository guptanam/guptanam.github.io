Beat the Beat up

USC Games Expo (2021)

Specifications

  • Duration: July 2020 - Present.
  • Team size: 40
  • Platform: Oculus Quest/Rift.
  • Type: USC Advanced Games Project (AGP).

Concept

Beat the Beat up is a VR action/rhythm game where combat is evocative of Bollywood dance and gameplay syncs with music.

My Contribution

  • Lead Engineer.
  • Designs game-mechanics, QuickTime events and combat.
  • Solves challenging and complex problems around refining game logic for high-quality gameplay .
  • Programs new systems and tools to facilitate creation of exciting new content by members of Design team.
  • Assists in developing project plans and commitments.
  • Collaborates closely with design, art and production teams to access different game systems and create pipelines across the departments for key game deliverables.
  • Participates in the interviewing of new Engineers for our team.
  • Playtests frequently to polish gameplay.
