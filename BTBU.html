Beat the Beat up

Specifications

  • Duration: July 2020 - Present.
  • Team size: 27
  • Platform: Oculus Quest.
  • Type: USC Advanced Games Project (AGP).

Concept

Beat the Beat up is a VR action/rhythm game where combat is evocative of Bollywood dance and gameplay syncs with music. It is an Advanced Games Project due to be presented at the USC Games Expo in May' 21.
The player will fight enemies (physically) on the beats of music by reacting to QuickTime events. These events would serve as signals as to what attack (punch, slap, grab) the player should use, when should he attack and where (enemy's body parts) should he attack the enemy. In this fashion, the player will play 1 complete Bollywood song (1 level).

I coded an event handler to facilitate QuickTime events . This event handler reads information from a csv file and corectly syncs events with music. This csv file contains information about the events which includes the following fields: hit-time, attack-type, hit-location and targetted-enemy.

VR physics, particulary combat, is a major part of our game and this area falls under my belt. I am the chief-architect for combat and related gameplay features. I engineered the grab-and-throw ability by designing ragdolls for enemies. The player will use their VR controllers to grab and lift the enemy (the enemy here goes into a ragdoll state). The player would then be able to throw him our bang two enemies' heads and whatnot.
